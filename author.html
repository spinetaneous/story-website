Dr. Bob Burger XIV is a #1 New York Times best-selling author, Professor of Pediatrics, and Director of Orthopedics at Good Samaritan Medical Institutions. He is the author of three best-selling books: Live Large, The World is Your Oyster, and Everyone is a Maker. He serves on the board of Colgate, QUESTABOX, Google, Supreme, Costco, and many others. The author or coauthor of 15 books, Dr. Bob Burger XIV has sold over 15 million copies after earning his medical degree from Yale in 2013. He currently resides in Davis, California with his wife and three children.
